About This Collection

Rather than just reading about JavaScript, I build things. This collection represents my learning-by-doing approach ‚Äî every mini project targets a specific concept I want to understand deeply, not just skim.

Each project is intentionally small enough to finish in a day or two, but complex enough to force me to look things up, make mistakes, and figure things out. That cycle is the real learning.

As I progress through JavaScript ‚Äî from DOM manipulation into async programming, APIs, and eventually frameworks ‚Äî this collection will keep growing.

My Approach

I build each project first, then refactor. The first pass is about making it work. The second pass is about making it clean. This habit is already making me a more thoughtful developer.
